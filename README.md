# 💳 Credit Card Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Credit Card Analysis Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze customer behavior, transaction performance, revenue trends, and key business metrics. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor credit card operations, identify customer spending patterns, and make data-driven business decisions.

The report consists of **two interactive dashboard pages**:

* **Credit Card Transaction Dashboard**
* **Credit Card Customer Dashboard**

Together, these dashboards provide a complete overview of transaction performance and customer insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall credit card revenue and transaction performance.
* Track customer spending behavior across different segments.
* Monitor transaction trends over time.
* Identify high-value customers and profitable customer groups.
* Compare revenue by different customer demographics.
* Support business decisions using interactive visualizations.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Credit Card Transaction Dashboard

This dashboard focuses on transaction-level analysis.

### Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Transaction Amount
* Total Interest Earned
* Total Transaction Count
* Average Transaction Value
* Revenue Growth
* Quarterly / Monthly Performance

### Business Insights

* Revenue trends over time
* Transaction volume analysis
* Interest earned analysis
* High-performing transaction categories
* Spending distribution
* Revenue contribution by different card categories

---

## 2️⃣ Credit Card Customer Dashboard

This dashboard provides customer-level analysis.

### Customer Insights

* Revenue by Gender
* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Income Group
* Revenue by Education
* Revenue by Occupation
* Revenue by Marital Status
* Revenue by State
* Customer Segmentation

### Business Benefits

* Identify profitable customer segments
* Understand customer demographics
* Improve targeted marketing
* Support customer retention strategies
* Optimize business growth decisions
